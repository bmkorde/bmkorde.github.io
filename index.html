Effects of Environmental Indicators on Rice Price Volatility in India

proj-02

Machine learning project to understand how air pollution and climate change affect rice prices in India. Tested four models — Linear Regression, Time Series Regression, Random Forest, and XGBoost — with XGBoost performing best. Shows how environmental factors impact food prices to support early warnings and planning.

Predicting BBL Match Outcomes from Historical Match Data

proj-03

A machine learning model to predict Big Bash League (BBL) match outcomes using mid-innings data. Enhanced Logistic Regression and Decision Tree models with player role and game situation features to significantly improve prediction accuracy. Demonstrates how combining match context with player impact captures T20 cricket dynamics.
